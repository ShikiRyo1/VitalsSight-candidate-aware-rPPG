# Evidence boundaries

## Confirmatory and primary evidence

- Internal primary contrast: V32 causal candidate path versus the prior within-study source-preserving selector under participant-disjoint nested development.
- External primary test: the prospectively frozen V31 candidate-aware selector evaluated once on the untouched EMPD cohort.
- Statistical unit: participant. Window-pooled values are descriptive unless explicitly identified otherwise.

## Exploratory evidence

- V32 temporal-path increment versus independent emission.
- V32 fixed-model EMPD replay, because EMPD outcomes were known before V32 was designed.
- Candidate-oracle and regret analyses, because reference HR defines the oracle and never enters inference.
- Release/review metrics, because the research score is not a calibrated safety probability and release coverage is sparse.

## Claims not established

- Universal state-of-the-art performance.
- A calibrated probability of safety or correctness.
- Autonomous clinical release, clinical utility, or end-to-end real-time deployment.
- Generalization of the post hoc V32 replay as an untouched external validation.

## Data and media boundary

Only aggregate outputs are packaged. Identifiable frames, raw media, participant-level EMPD tables, window-level reference HR, demographics, conditions, source IDs and timestamps are excluded. Dataset access and publication rights remain governed by the original providers.
