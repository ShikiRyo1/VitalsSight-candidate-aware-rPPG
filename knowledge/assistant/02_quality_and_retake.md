# Acquisition quality and retake / 采集质量与重拍

## Minimum recording requirements / 最低录制要求

The current research workflow requires a decodable supported video, at least 8 seconds of usable duration, at least 15 frames per second, and at least 320 by 240 pixels. A 20 to 30 second recording, at least 20 frames per second, and 480p or higher are preferred. The full face should remain centered and visible under even frontal illumination, with the head and camera held steady.

当前研究流程要求视频格式可解码、可用时长至少 8 秒、帧率至少 15 fps，分辨率至少 320×240。推荐录制 20–30 秒、至少 20 fps，并使用 480p 或更高分辨率。完整人脸应在均匀正面光照下保持居中可见，头部与相机尽量稳定。

## Why retake happens / 为什么需要重拍

Retake means the acquisition evidence did not support entry into, or reliable completion of, candidate analysis. Typical triggers include insufficient duration, unreadable input, very low face visibility, severe illumination failure, or another failed preflight requirement. When preflight fails, candidate construction is recorded as not entered; a missing candidate count is not presented as an independent acquisition failure.

重拍表示采集证据不足以支持进入候选分析，或不足以可靠完成分析。常见触发项包括时长不足、输入不可读、人脸可见度过低、严重光照失败或其他预检查不通过。预检查失败时，系统会记录“未进入候选构建”；此时缺少候选数量不会被描述为另一个独立采集失败。

## Corrective actions / 纠正方法

Correct only the checks that failed or warned. Reposition the face if visibility is low, use even frontal lighting if illumination is weak, stabilize the head and camera if motion is high, and extend the recording if duration is insufficient. After correction, repeat the preflight and verify that the affected check passes before relying on candidate evidence.

只纠正真实失败或警告的项目。人脸可见度低时重新构图；光照不足时改为均匀正面光；运动过大时稳定头部和相机；时长不足时延长录制。纠正后重新进行预检查，并在依赖候选证据前确认相关检查已经通过。
