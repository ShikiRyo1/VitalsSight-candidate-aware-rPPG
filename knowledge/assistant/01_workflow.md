# VitalsSight workflow / VitalsSight 工作流程

## Input and consent / 输入与授权

VitalsSight accepts a local RGB facial video for retrospective research processing. Before analysis, the operator records the research purpose, confirms processing consent, and selects the raw-video retention policy. The recommended policy deletes the uploaded raw video after analysis and retains only derived evidence, the output state, the report, and the audit trail.

VitalsSight 接收本地 RGB 人脸视频用于回顾性研究处理。分析前，操作员需要记录研究目的、确认处理授权，并选择原始视频保留策略。推荐策略是在分析后删除上传的原始视频，仅保留派生证据、输出状态、报告和审计记录。

## Four-stage path / 四阶段路径

1. Prepare: confirm purpose, consent, framing, illumination, duration, and stability.
2. Qualify: check readability, duration, frame rate, resolution, illumination, motion, and face visibility.
3. Analyze: construct regional and algorithm-specific HR candidates, compare their support, and retain route identity.
4. Act: return release, review, or retake with an evidence packet and an operational next step.

1. 准备：确认目的、授权、构图、光照、时长和稳定性。
2. 质检：检查可读性、时长、帧率、分辨率、光照、运动和人脸可见度。
3. 分析：构建区域与算法特异的心率候选，比较支持度并保留来源身份。
4. 处理：返回放行、复核或重采状态，同时给出证据包和可执行下一步。

## What the assistant can do / 助手能做什么

The assistant can explain recorded evidence, summarize a report, locate failed checks, retrieve local workflow guidance, navigate to a workspace, and prepare a review update for explicit confirmation. It cannot change the measured HR, override the output gate, diagnose a condition, recommend treatment, or silently update a review.

助手可以解释已记录证据、概述报告、定位失败检查、检索本地流程指引、跳转工作区，并准备一个等待明确确认的复核更新。助手不能改变测量心率、覆盖输出门控、诊断疾病、建议治疗或静默修改复核记录。
