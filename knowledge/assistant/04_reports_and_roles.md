# Reports, roles, and follow-up / 报告、角色与后续处理

## Evidence report / 证据报告

The report contains the output state, published HR only when released, acquisition-gate state, policy and model identity, operational interpretation, evidence-to-action chain, candidate branches, implementation provenance, review record, audit trail, and the research claim boundary. PDF is intended for human reading; JSON preserves the complete machine-readable contract; Markdown supports review; CSV contains the case-level row.

报告包括输出状态、仅在放行时显示的已发布心率、采集门控状态、策略与模型身份、操作解释、证据到行动链、候选分支、实现溯源、复核记录、审计记录和研究声明边界。PDF 用于人工阅读；JSON 保留完整机器可读契约；Markdown 便于复核；CSV 提供案例级数据行。

## Capture operator / 采集操作员

The capture operator confirms consent and purpose, follows framing and lighting guidance, runs the assessment, and responds to retake instructions. The operator should not reinterpret candidate values as a final HR when the state is review or retake.

采集操作员确认授权与目的，遵循构图和光照指引，运行评估，并执行重拍要求。状态为复核或重采时，操作员不得把候选值重新解释为最终心率。

## Evidence reviewer / 证据复核人员

The reviewer checks the reason, candidate evidence, quality factors, policy identity, and audit trail. A review update requires status, priority, assignee, note, and resolution where applicable. Assistant-prepared updates remain pending until a human explicitly confirms them.

复核人员检查原因、候选证据、质量因素、策略身份和审计记录。复核更新应在适用时填写状态、优先级、负责人、备注和处理结果。助手准备的更新在人工明确确认前始终处于待处理状态。

## Clinician and administrator / 医护人员与管理员

The clinician-facing explanation summarizes the research evidence and limitations without providing diagnosis or treatment. The administrator checks service health, model availability, action-policy configuration, data retention, audit completeness, and fallback behavior.

面向医护人员的解释用于概述研究证据和限制，不提供诊断或治疗建议。管理员负责检查服务健康状态、模型可用性、动作策略配置、数据保留、审计完整性和降级行为。
